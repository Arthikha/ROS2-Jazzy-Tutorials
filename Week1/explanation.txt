\[

\begin{bmatrix}

\sin\theta & \cos\theta

For \(\theta = 90^\circ\):

\[

\begin{bmatrix}
\cos 90^\circ & -\sin 90^\circ \\
\sin 90^\circ & \cos 90^\circ
